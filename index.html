OSCI—The temple of learning

Oxford Skill & Coaching Institute (OSCI) lies on the famed NTR road—linking the world-famed winter tourist resort of Gulmarg to the rest of the country. The Institute is located ideally, near Govt Higher Secondary School, Chandilora—making it convenient and easily accessible for the learners, who come (to the Institute) in droves from GHSS, Govt. Degree College ( Chandilora) and other educational Institutes in the adjoining parts. As the northern part of Kashmir—Chandilora—has emerged as the den of coaching Centers at unregulated pace, I took upon this huge obligation of chipping in with my contribution—an effort which led to the establishment of OCI in 2017. The sole motto and mantra of starting OCI was to transform the way education is imparted in all the coaching Centers – in the vicinity and the adjoining hubs. Being a strong votary of quality education, I am heaving a huge sigh of relief, basking in the glory of this undisputed fact that my Institute has lived upto its billing by disseminating affordable and quality education, despite being in the naïve stage of operations. Catering to the needs of students’ from diverse backgrounds, OCI has carved a unique niche among the galore of coaching Institutes, functioning here. The speedy success of the Institute has become worth-emulating for its counterparts (in the area) as I have set the ball of quality education rolling right from the day of inception of my societal obligation. Many years now into my operation at the OCI, I am swelling with pride that the perennial coaching imparted in our Institute has ushered in a new era of quality education and produced overwhelming outcome. It is pertinent to mention here that students’ appearing in Board exams for class 8th, 10th, 11th and 12th (in Arts and Science stream) have passed their exams with flying colours, attesting my efforts of turning a new leaf in their learning and understanding skills. The overall satisfaction exuded and expressed by the learners’ ( enrolled at OCI) is endorsement of my efforts to promote best educational and technological know-how, cutting across the diversity of prerequisites in today’s competitive and cut-throat educational ambience. At the conclusion, I declare with unwavering commitment that OCI will work for the betterment of community as a whole in Tangmarg, and other adjoining parts by complementing the school education of the wards with quality result-oriented and effective coaching.
