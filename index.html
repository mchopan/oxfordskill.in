CEO OF OSCI MUDASIR AHMAD CHOPAN

With the proliferation of diversity in education penetrating at promising levels, It is a huge onus to shoulder the responsibility of providing quality academic and skill based education among the learners’ in our locality. Subsequently, we are driven by the desire of deriving the best out of learners’ cashing in on the creative aspect of learning. The mantra of our learning Centre hinges on adorning learners’ with the requisite learning skills as well as quenching their thirst of taking on the world with knowledge and technological know-how. This acts as the guiding force for my team as we are wedded to the concept of preserving quality in the din of quantity galore. Education has diversified with the onset of digital revolution across the world. Apart from blessing the students with academic knowledge, our learning Centre also contributes in honing the skills of local girls’ by making them conversant with various skill based courses promoted by the Government of India.
